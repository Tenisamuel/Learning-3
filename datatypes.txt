Basic Data Types

    Integers (int):
        Represents whole numbers, positive or negative, without a fractional part.
        Example: 5, -3

    Floating Point Numbers (float):
        Represents real numbers, including decimals.
        Example: 3.14, -0.001

    Strings (str):
        Represents textual data, enclosed in single, double, or triple quotes.
        Example: 'Hello', "Python Programming"

    Booleans (bool):
        Represents one of two values: True or False.
        Often used in conditions and logical operations.
